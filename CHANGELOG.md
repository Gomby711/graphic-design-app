# Changelog

All notable changes to Graphic Design Tips are documented here. The in-app
"What's changed" panel (next to the version number in the top bar) always
shows the most recent entry below.

## 2026-07-21
- Added instructional demo gifs for the last 3 Advanced Photoshop lessons (HDR Merge & Tone Mapping, Neural Filters Deep Dive, and Advanced Puppet Warp) that were missing them.
- Replaced the generic emoji icons shown in the collapsed sidebar with recognizable app-branded icons: a Photoshop "Ps" badge, an After Effects "Ae" badge, a real Figma logo, and a gold "A" badge for Advanced Photoshop Lessons.

## 2026-07-18 (v1.0.8)
- Added 6 new **Motion Design in Adobe After Effects** lessons: expressions (wiggle/loopOut), the Puppet Pin tool & character rigging, 3D layers & the Camera tool, motion tracking with the Tracker panel, the Render Queue & Media Encoder export workflow, and the Essential Graphics panel & Motion Graphics Templates (Mogrts) — each with instructional gifs.
- Added 8 new **Advanced Photoshop Lessons**, including two dedicated compositing lessons (CGI/3D render compositing, and matching grain/chromatic aberration/vignette for composite realism), plus advanced channel masking, advanced Liquify & warp, panorama/Photomerge stitching, HDR merge & tone mapping, a Neural Filters deep dive, and advanced Puppet Warp for pose changes.
- Added a collapsible sidebar with per-section icons, so you can shrink the lesson list down to an icon rail for more reading space.
- Fixed multi-letter lesson badges (like AP-1, AE-6, AI-3) cramming onto two lines in the sidebar — they now stay on one line.
- Removed a stray empty version-pill icon that could appear next to "by Evan Taye Lee".

## 2026-07-18
- Added the **Advanced Photoshop Lessons** section, right below Photoshop Lessons: 10 new lessons covering compositing, luminosity masks, frequency separation, sky replacement, double exposure, shadow/light/perspective matching, photorealistic water reflections, green screen spill removal, Camera Raw color grading, and Smart Object workflows — each with a real 1080p HD YouTube video and instructional gifs.
- Moved the **Motion Design in Adobe After Effects** section above Figma Lessons in the sidebar.
- Fixed section heading text color across every lesson — headings now use the app's blue accent instead of the old scarlet-orange.
- Added an in-app version number with a "What's changed" panel so you can always confirm the app is up to date and see recent release notes without leaving the app.

## Earlier releases
- Fixed auto-update never actually relaunching the app after downloading an update.
- Fixed a silent auto-update failure on Windows.
- Added a fullscreen lightbox for lesson gifs, 4 new Photoshop lessons, and a full After Effects motion design course.
